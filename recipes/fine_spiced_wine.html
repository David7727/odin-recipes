Fine Spiced Wine

Description

This spices wine recipe is very old. The wine can be further mixed with honey and other ingredients to make Honey refresher for travellers.

Ingredients

  • 6 SEXTARII OF HONEY
  • 2 SEXTARII OF WINE
  • A DASH OF COLD WINE
  • 4 OZS OF CRUSHED PEPPER
  • 3 SCRUPLES OF MASTICH
  • A DRACHM OF LAUREL LEAVES
  • A DRACHM OF SAFFRON
  • 5 DRACHMS OF ROASTED DATE STONES CRUSHED AND PREVIOUSLY SOAKED IN WINE
  • 18 SEXTARII OF LIGHT WINE
  • CRUSHED CHARCOAL (OPTIONAL)

Steps

  1. INTO A COPPER BOWL PUT 6 SEXTARII OF HONEY AND 2 SEXTARII OF WINE
  2. HEAT ON A SLOW FIRE, CONSTANTLY STIRRING THE MIXTURE WITH A WHIP
  3. AT THE BOILING POINT ADD A DASH OF COLD WINE, RETIRE FROM STOVE AND SKIM
  4. REPEAT THIS TWICE OR THREE TIMES, LET IT REST TILL THE NEXT DAY, AND SKIM AGAIN
  5. THEN ADD 4 OZS. OF CRUSHED PEPPER, 3 SCRUPLES OF MASTICH, A DRACHM EACH OF LAUREL LEAVES AND SAFFRON, 5 DRACHMS OF ROASTED DATE STONES CRUSHED AND PREVIOUSLY SOAKED IN WINE TO SOFTEN THEM
  6. WHEN THIS IS PROPERLY DONE ADD 18 SEXTARII OF LIGHT WINE
  7. TO CLARIFY IT PERFECTLY, ADD CRUSHED CHARCOAL TWICE OR AS OFTEN AS NECESSARY WHICH WILL DRAW THE RESIDUE TOGETHER
