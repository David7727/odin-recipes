Rose Wine

Description

An old recipe for making rose wine. Somilar technique can be applied to make violet wine from fresh violets

Ingredients

  • ROSE PETALS
  • WINE
  • HONEY

Steps

  1. SEW ROSE PETALS, THE LOWER WHITE PART REMOVED INTO A LINEN BAG
  2. IMMERSE IN WINE FOR SEVEN DAYS
  3. THEREUPON ADD A SACK OF NEW PETALS WHICH ALLOW TO DRAW FOR ANOTHER SEVEN DAYS
  4. AGAIN REMOVE THE OLD PETALS AND REPLACE THEM BY FRESH ONES FOR ANOTHER WEEK
  5. STRAIN THE WINE THROUGH THE COLANDER
  6. BEFORE SERVING, ADD HONEY SWEETENING TO TASTE
